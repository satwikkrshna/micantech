MICAN EXPANDS ITS PORTFOLIO IN 2022

Mican Technologies, a leading provider of IT solutions, is pleased to announce the addition of several new clients to our portfolio. We are excited to partner with:

VSP, a Global Vision Care Company CSX, a Leading North America Rail-based Freight Transportation Supplier

Christus Health, a Global Faith-Based Team of Healthcare Professionals

Crowley Logistics, a Privately held, U.S. owned and operated Logistics, Marine and Energy Company

Vac-con, a Global Provider of Custom-made Combination Sewer Cleaning, Hydro-Excavation, Industrial Vacuum and High-Pressure Water Jetting Equipment.

“Adding these new clients to our portfolio is a testament to our expertise and dedication to delivering value to our clients,” said Siva Kanumuri, Vice President of Mican Technologies. “We are proud to work with such reputable organizations and are committed to providing them with exceptional service and support.”

Our team of professionals is dedicated to developing customized solutions that meet the specific needs of each client. Whether it’s implementing a new system, providing ongoing maintenance and support, or helping clients navigate complex regulatory requirements, we are committed to delivering quality solutions that help our clients achieve their goals.
